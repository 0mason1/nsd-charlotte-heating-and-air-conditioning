About Us

Charlotte's Hometown HVAC Company Since 2004

Charlotte Heating and Air Conditioning was founded right here in the Queen City by a family of HVAC professionals who believe your comfort should never be compromised. For over two decades, we've been the go-to choice for homeowners across Charlotte, Ballantyne, Huntersville, Concord, and beyond.

Our team of NATE-certified technicians brings decades of combined experience to every job, from routine tune-ups to complete system replacements. We treat every home like it's our own — because we're your neighbors.
